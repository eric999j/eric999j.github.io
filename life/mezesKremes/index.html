Recipe

Filling:

  • 0.5 cups milk

  • 5 tbsp semolina (打碎粗糧）

  • 0.5 tsp vanilla extract

  • 50 g butter

  • 60 g powdered sugar

  • juice of a half lemon

  • 200 g apricot jam (中層）

  1. Pour 0.5 cups milk in a saucepan.
  2. Add 0.5 tsp vanilla extract and bring it to a boil.
  3. Stirring continuously and remove from the heat until it thickens.
  4. Put 50 g butter in a separate bowl, add 60g powdered sugar and beat with a mixer until fluffy.
  5. Combine creamy butter with the semolina pudding adding lemon zest and juice.

Dough



  • 50 g powdered sugar (25g糖與25g玉米粉在果汁機打碎）
  • 1 egg (忘了加)
  • 20 g butter
  • 1 tbsp honey（橘子蜜）
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 200 g flour（打碎燕麥片）
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  1. Place salt, 1tsp honey, 2tsp milk and 20 g butter in another saucepan and heat them up (but don’t boil)
  2. Add 200 g flour, 0.5 tsp baking soda and 1 egg.
  3. Knead into a smooth dough and then divide into four equal parts and covered it prevents cooling.
  4. Roll out the first ball into a paper thin (1-2 mm thin) square and poke with a fork .
  5. Bake for 4-5 minutes. Repeat the procedure with the three remaining balls.

Assemble

  • fourth layer
  • rest of the filling
  • third layer
  • apricot jam
  • second layer
  • half of the semolina cream
  • first layer

Glaze


  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder（杏仁粉）
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 25 g butter
  • 1.5 tbsp water
  1. In a small pot bring powdered sugar, cocoa powder and water to a boil.
  2. Remove from the heat and immediately stir in butter.
  3. Pour the cocoa glaze over the cake and spread evenly.
  4. Stay in the fridge overnight before cutting.
