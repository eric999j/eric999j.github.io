葡萄牙文初探

Posted on 2022-07-03 Edited on 2022-08-07 In 生活 Views:
Symbols count in article: 1.8k Reading time ≈ 2 mins.

從我們的環遊世界知道中南美洲只有巴西說葡萄牙文，這也讓我想了解這門語言。

既然巴西友人很熱心的教我，我就好好練習吧。

  • ü: Don’t use it often
  • Special single word: E(And), É(Is)
  • Right single word: à. À is used alone like “eu vou à escola” means “I go to school” in this case à means “to”
  • Left: á,é,í,ó,ú use in words, stressed syllable (where the sound is strongest), céu(Sky)
