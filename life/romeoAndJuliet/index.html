Hi Brazil!

It’s a simple dish, you can buy guava paste,queso fresco in Brazil, but I don’t find those things in Taiwan, but my Brazilian chef said I can make by myself.

History

Colombia,Venezuela: Bocadillo Con Queso

Cheese and Guava Paste
Going north to this is not much drama, the ingredients are fixed guava.

Brazi: Romeu e Julieta

Cheese and Guava Paste
Romeo and Juliet was written by William Shakespeare in late 16th century, Columbus came to America in the 15th, so it make sense, I guess after the 20th century Brazilians named this southern dessert after an opera.

Paraguay: Dulce de Guayaba

Cheese and Guava Paste
From here change to Guava paste.

Uruguay: Martín Fierro

Cheese and Quince Paste
Martín Fierro loves Vigilante, and change sweet potato paste with quince paste.

Argentina: Vigilante

Cheese and Sweet-Potato Paste
Guava is not a fruit endemic of Argentina, but Argentine use sweet potato and call Postre Vigilante (or Martín Fierro).

The story behind this dessert is that it was created in a 1920’s Palermo cantina (cafeteria/snack bar) that was frequented by police officers, and for this reason received the name “vigilante”.
ref

Recipe

Guava Paste


Remove 2 guavas‘ skin and seeds, deep seeds into 1 cup of water.


Add the soaked seed sticky water, add 2 cups of sugar and cook until thick(can add some gelatin too),


Refrigerate

Cottage Cheese

Cottage cheese is more easy to make than mozzarella.


Fresh milk, vinegar, any kind of cheese, put 1 cup milk to boil, then add 1 pinch of salt or parmesan cheese or any cheese, mix well, starts to boil.


Put 2 tablespoons of vinegar into the hot milk.


Wait a while and pass all the whey through the sieve

Combine
